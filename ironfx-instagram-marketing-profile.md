## 🎨 DESIGN PROFILE

### Brand Colors
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| IronFX Blue | `#003366` | Primary backgrounds, headers |
| Bright Blue | `#0066CC` | CTAs, highlights, buttons |
| Gold/Amber | `#F5A623` | Accents, premium feel, awards |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Text on dark, clean space |
| Dark Grey | `#1A1A2E` | Secondary backgrounds |
| Light Grey | `#F0F4F8` | Cards, subtle dividers |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold sans-serif (e.g. Montserrat Bold, Bebas Neue)
- **Body/Captions:** Clean sans-serif (e.g. Poppins Regular, Inter)
- **Numbers/Data:** Monospaced or tabular for stats/prices

### Visual Style
- **Aesthetic:** Professional, premium, financial — not playful
- **Imagery:** Trading charts, globe/world maps, upward momentum visuals, lifestyle of successful traders
- **Icons:** Clean line icons, financial symbols, platform screenshots
- **Layout:** Strong grid, left-heavy or centered composition, minimal clutter
- **Photography:** Professionals at screens, confident people, skylines (London/Dubai/NY), handshakes
- **Avoid:** Stock-looking cheesy images, generic finance clipart

### Post Templates Needed
1. 📈 **Market Update** — chart snapshot + key stat
2. 🏆 **Award/Achievement** — dark background + gold accent
3. 💡 **Trading Tip** — carousel, clean edu-style
4. 🌍 **Regional Promo** — geo-targeted offer card
5. 📊 **Infographic** — data-heavy, multiple panels
6. 🎥 **Reel Cover** — bold text overlay on video thumbnail
7. 💬 **Quote/Testimonial** — broker/client social proof
8. 📣 **Event/Webinar** — event promo card

## 📝 CONTENT PROFILE

### Content Pillars (The 5 Ps)
| Pillar | % of Content | What It Looks Like |
|---|---|---|
| **Performance** | 25% | Market updates, instrument spotlights, weekly/daily analysis |
| **Platform** | 20% | MT4/MT5 tips, app features, platform walkthroughs |
| **People** | 15% | Trader stories, team spotlights, client wins |
| **Promo** | 20% | Bonuses, spreads, account types, regional offers |
| **Power (Education)** | 20% | How-to trading, glossary, risk management, strategies |

### Content Formats
| Format | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Static Posts | 3–4x/week | Brand awareness, promos, tips |
| Carousels | 2x/week | Education, multi-step guides |
| Reels (15–30s) | 3x/week | Reach, engagement, platform demos |
| Stories | Daily | Polls, Q&A, market alerts, CTAs |
| Live (IG Live) | 1x/month | Webinar-style, analyst Q&A |

### Caption Style
- **Tone:** Confident, clear, professional — not salesy
- **Hook:** Always open with a bold statement or question
- **Structure:** Hook → Value → CTA
- **Length:** 100–180 words for posts, short for Reels
- **Hashtags:** 5–10 per post (mix of branded + niche)
  - Branded: `#IronFX` `#IronFXTrader`
  - Niche: `#ForexTrading` `#CFDTrading` `#MT4` `#MT5` `#TradingLife` `#ForexSignals` `#CurrencyTrading`

### CTA Examples
- "Open a free demo account → link in bio"
- "Start trading with as low as $100 → link in bio"
- "Learn more about [instrument] → swipe up / link in bio"
